03. About

Pedro B. Abreu is a film and advertising director working where comedy, emotion and cinematic craft collide.

Trained at ESCAC, Pedro has directed feature films, short films, series, music videos and campaigns for international brands. His debut feature, Blue Rai, won the Silver Biznaga - Audience Award in Zonazine at Malaga Film Festival, and his work has screened at Cannes Short Film Corner, Goteborg Film Festival, Notodofilmfest, El Sol Bilbao and numerous film and audiovisual innovation festivals.

His work combines advertising rhythm, actor-driven storytelling and a natural instinct for characters who experience the absurd with complete emotional truth.
